Risotto

Description:

Vegetable stock risotto with mushroom and Parmesan chesse.

Ingredients:

  • Mushrooms: 350 g
  • Onion: 1 piece(s)
  • Garlic: 3 cloves
  • Risotto Rice: 1 cup
  • Parmesan: 120 g
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Chop the ingredients.
  2. Add 1/4 of mushrooms and onions and 1 garlic clove to a pot, add water and boil to get stock.
  3. In a pan: Fry onion, wait, add garlic, add mushrooms.
  4. Add the rice to the pan and slowly add the stock.
  5. Season with salt and pepper. Consider that Parmesan will be added in the next step.
  6. When the consistency of the rice is good enough, add the parmesan, mix. Remove from heat and let it rest.
