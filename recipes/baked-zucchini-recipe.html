Baked Zucchini

Description:

Oven baked zucchini with a Frishkäse, onion, corn and bacon.

Ingredients:

  • Zucchini: 2-4 piece(s)
  • Onion: 1 piece(s)
  • Corn: 1 can(s) or 2 piece(s)
  • Bacon: 250 g
  • Corn: 1 can or 2 piece(s)
  • Frishkäse: As desired
  • Salt & Pepper

Instructions:

  1. Cut the zucchini in half, place inside the oven for around half an hour.
  2. Heat oil in a pan, add the bacon.
  3. Chop the onion and fry in the pan until transparency.
  4. Bacon: 250 g
  5. Add the corn and cook for a while.
  6. When the zucchini is ready, take it out, season with salt and pepper.
  7. Spread the Frischkäse and put the other cooked ingredients on top.
