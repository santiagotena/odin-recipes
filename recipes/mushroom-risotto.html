Mushroom Risotto

Ingredients

Mushrooms 350 gr
Onion 1 piece
Garlic 3 cloves
Risotto rice 1 cup
Parmesan 100 gr

Instructions

  1. Chop the ingredients.
  2. Add 1/4 of mushrooms and onions and 1 garlic clove to a pot, add water and boil to get stock.
  3. In a pan: Fry onion, wait, add garlic, add mushrooms.
  4. Add the rice to the pan and slowly add the stock.
  5. When the consistency of the rice is good enough, add the parmesan, mix. Remove from heat and let it rest.
