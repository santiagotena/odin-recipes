Cream Chicken

Ingredients

Chicken breasts 500 gr
Carrots 3 or 4 pieces
Sweet Corn 2 pieces
Tomatoes 2 pieces
Salt 1 tsp
Black pepper 1/4 tsp
Tumeric (Kurkuma) 1/4 tsp
Paprika 1/2 tsp
Garlic Powder 1/4 tsp
Sour Cream 200 gr

Instructions

  1. Cut the chicken into cubes.
  2. Cook them in olive oil for 3 to 4 minutes.
  3. Peel and slice carrots into thing circles.
  4. Flip the chicken and add the carrots. Cover and simmer for 5 minutes.
  5. Add corn, cover and simmer for 3 to 4 minutes.
  6. Slice the tomatoes into cubes and add them into the pan.
  7. Season and mix. Let simmer for 2 minutes.
  8. Add the cream. Simmer for 2 minutes.
